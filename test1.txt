The university is the major seat of the Congregation of Holy Cross ( albeit not its official headquarters , which are in Rome ) . Its main seminary , Moreau Seminary , is located on the campus across St. Joseph lake from the Main Building . Old College , the oldest building on campus and located near the shore of St. Mary lake , houses undergraduate seminarians . Retired priests and brothers reside in Fatima House ( a former retreat center ) , Holy Cross House , as well as Columba Hall near the Grotto . The university through the Moreau Seminary has ties to theologian Frederick Buechner .
